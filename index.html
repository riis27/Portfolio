Get to Know Rissa

As a perennially curious spirit, Rissa has a wide array of colors on her palette of interests and skills; and just like a painting, finds that all her experiences translate into vibrant hues that are embedded into anything she creates from the stroke of her hand--whether by paintbrush, or keyboard. From working as a teacher, to an event curator, community volunteer, photographer and art curator, to writing and now to web development, she utilizes her talents to bring any vision to vivid life.
